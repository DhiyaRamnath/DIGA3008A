Nana

The farm smells of petrichor and freshly turned earth, of grandmother's scones cooling on the windowsill. Through a child's eyes, we witness the quiet magic of a grandfather who hears stories in cows' lowing and finds companionship in oak trees. This tender memory explores how love lingers in places long after people are gone in the ring of a gold bell, in the whistle of wind through leaves that sounds almost like a familiar hum.

Revolting's Revolt

This is a story about the hunger to be seen not with pity, but with recognition. When a chance encounter with a wealthy stranger ignites an obsession in a homeless man named "Revolting", we're forced to ask: What does it cost to reclaim your humanity in a world that treats you as debris?

Lover's Gambit

Jocelyn prepares a romantic anniversary dinner, complete with candlelight, wine, and a hidden agenda. When a blonde hair clings to her husband's scarf, their chess game takes on a dangerous new dimension. Every move exposes deeper lies. Checkmate is inevitable... but who will surrender first?

The Ink of a Feather Pen

Behind latticed windows scented with dukhoon incense, Zara's fingers itch for the forbidden weight of a book. In a world where her wedding abaya is more valuable than her thoughts, ink becomes her rebellion. This lyrical tale whispers of the dangerous alchemy that occurs when a woman discovers the power of words and the terrifying freedom that comes when she decides to write her own ending.
