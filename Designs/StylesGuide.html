Style Guide

All the final chosen elements for the design of my website

Color Pallete

I work with a warm, earthy color scheme. The main colors are #876d64 (a muted brown) for primary elements, #f6ece3 (an off-white) for backgrounds, and #c59d9b (a soft pinkish-brown) for hover states. These colors create a cohesive, professional but creative look.

Fonts

I use Times New Roman for headings to give a classic, literary feel, and Montserrat for body text because it's clean and readable. The font sizes are consistent across all pages, with good contrast against backgrounds.

Logo Variations

My logo appears in two versions - a full version for headers and a simplified one for footers. Both use the same color scheme and maintain clarity at different sizes. They're always placed where users expect to find them.

Button Designs

All buttons follow the same rules: rounded corners, consistent padding, and clear hover states. Primary buttons use our main brown color with light text, switching to the pinkish-brown on hover.

Visual Elements

This includes the decorative branches that frame some pages, the floral patterns used in backgrounds, and any other graphical touches. They're all semi-transparent to keep the focus on content while adding visual interest.

Icon Designs

Where icons are needed, they're simple and recognizable. They match our color scheme and are sized consistently. Hover effects are applied where appropriate to show they're interactive.
